Which subtle weather mood fits ConstructAsk?

All three keep the dashboard readable. The difference is how much the weather scene calls attention to itself in the hero.

A

Sunlit Site Drift

Recommended. Warm sunrise, tiny birds, light storm edge, very thin rain on one side.

B

Weather Cycle

Sun remains visible, clouds gradually cover the view, rain appears briefly, then clears again.

C

Storm Signal

Darker cloud movement and more visible rain. Still subtle, but risk feels more urgent.
